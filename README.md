# SuperMart_Analysis_2014-2017
## Company Background
**SuperMart** is  a retail chain supermarket that operates across the United States offering a wide range of products including office supplies, furnitures, technology, and appliances. The company has 531 retail stores that operates across the United States with 10,000+ employees generating annual sales that grew from $484,000 in 2014 to $ 730,000 in 2017. 

Business growth, customers acqusition and retention have been the main goals to achieve in the company. By producing and analyzing clean transactional sales dataset, the company will be able to identify essential metrics and indicators to evaluate its performance in the marketplace.
