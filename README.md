# SuperMart_Analysis_2014-2017
## Company Background
**SuperMart** is  a retail chain supermarket that operates across the United States offering a wide range of products including office suppies, furniture, technology, and appliances. The company has 531 retail stores that operates across the United States with 10,000+ employees generating annual sales that grew from $484,000 in 2014 to $ 730,000 in 2017.
